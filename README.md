# Optera Onboarding Portal — BA Case Study

## Project Overview
An end-to-end Business Analysis engagement for Optera HR Technologies,
a B2B HRTech SaaS client operating across India and the US.
Optera approached TechBridge Solutions to build an Intelligent Customer 
Onboarding Portal, and I was assigned as the Business Analyst leading 
this engagement from initiation to delivery blueprint.

**Role:** Business Analyst — TechBridge Solutions  
**Client:** Optera HR Technologies  
**Status:** Ongoing  

---

## Business Problem
Optera's customer onboarding process was entirely manual,
scattered across emails, spreadsheets, and ad-hoc calls with 
no standardization, no tracking, and no clear ownership across 
teams. Customers across India and the US were not experiencing 
product value fast enough.

**The Ask:** Design and define an Intelligent Customer Onboarding 
Portal that standardizes the onboarding journey, tracks customer 
progress, and gives the CS team real-time visibility.

---

## My Role As BA
- Led end-to-end requirements gathering and stakeholder analysis
- Mapped current state process and designed future state solution
- Defined functional and non-functional requirements
- Created user stories and acceptance criteria for development
- Built data analysis layer and executive dashboard
- Delivered a complete product blueprint ready for development
